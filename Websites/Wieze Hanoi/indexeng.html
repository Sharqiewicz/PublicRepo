The issue of hanoi tower

Legend

According to the Tibetan legend, the monks in the temple of Brahma solve this puzzle for 64 discs. The legend says that when monks finish the task, the end of the world will come. Assuming that they follow the rules indicated in the legend, and that they perform 1 movement per second, the tower will take 264-1 = 18 446 744 073 709 551 615 (close to 18 and a half trillion) seconds, or about 584 billion years. For comparison: The Universe is around 13.7 billion years old ..

Towers of Hanoi - Rules

        
                
  1. The puzzle consists in rebuilding the tower from column A to column C
    2.             
  2. We have a discs with different sizes
    3.             
  3. They are stacked on the smallest to the largest
    4.             
  4. Only one disc can be moved at a time
    5.             
  5. You can not put a larger diameter disc on a smaller diameter disc

Algorithm

Solution for 1 element: AC

Solution for 2 elements: AB AC BA

Solution for 1 element: AC AB CB AC BA BC AC

As you can see, there is always AC traffic in the middle of the solution

Before the occurrence of the middle movement, we copy the moves from the previous solution and REPLACE LETTERS B with C the letter A remains unchanged.

After the middle traffic AC , we take the previous moves (from before AC ) and REPLACE LETTERS A with B the letter C remains unchanged.
